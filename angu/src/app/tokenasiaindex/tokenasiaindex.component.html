Index Strategy (How it is calculated)

The TokenAsia Index is a market-cap based index that measures the performance of the large-cap Tokensale market. The Index is currently made up of 10 members and serves as a representation for the performance of the market as a whole. It is calculated using CoinMarketCap data and updated every Tick. Read more...

It has three components:

  1. Market Capital
  2. Price
  3. Volume

Two Conditions to select the Tokens:

  1. Market Capital of 8.0 Billions or above.
  2. Daily volume of 450 Millions for the past 15 days.

So, Total no. of shortlisted currencies on the above basis are as follows:

  1. Bitcoin
  2. Ethereum
  3. Ripple
  4. Bitcoin Cash
  5. Litecoin

Index will list at 10000 points.

Market Capital

50% of the Index. In the ratio of their Market Capital. Rounded off to the lowest integer. So, Total Market Capital of above 5 Tokens = 5000 points

Price Points

Same ratio for each currency i.e 500 points each
Total of 2500 points.

Volume Points

Same Ratio for all the selected currencies.
500 points to each currency.
So total is 2500 Points.

Calculations

The value of the Index is calculated on every tick value according to the ratio decrease or increase of the above components accordingly.
